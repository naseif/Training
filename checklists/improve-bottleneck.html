Checklist for improving the throughput through a bottleneck

  1. What needs to be done that is not progressing as fast as you want it to?
  2. What do you need for that?
  3. Local optimization
    1. Are the people working as effective as they can on the job?
    2. Do they have enough resources available?
  4. You do not have other people / resources? Create free time around the bottleneck to get resources for free!
    1. What and who do you have that is waiting for the bottleneck to complete the work?
    2. What and who do you have at the front of the bottleneck, that does things that need to pass through the bottleneck?
  5. Maximize the time, the bottleneck resource has available
    1. What else does your bottleneck have to do?
      1. Can those tasks be given to other people?
      2. Are the other tasks really so important that they need to be done now?
  6. Maximize ability
    1. How can we improve the effectiveness of the time?
      1. Now?
        (Can we add internal/external help? Are there people that wait for the results and thus have free time with which they could help?)
      2. In the long term?
        Education, Teachers, Hiring, Automation?
