Checklist for improving the throughput through a bottleneck

  1. What needs to be done that is not progressing as fast as you want it to?
  2. What resources/people do you need for that?
  3. Optimize locally
    1. Are the current people working as effective as they can on the job?
    2. Do they have enough resources available?
  4. Maximize the time, the resources/people have available:
    1. What else do the people have to do?
      1. Can those tasks be given to other people?
      2. Are the other tasks really so important that they need to be done now?
  5. Maximize ability:
    1. How can we improve the effectiveness of the time?
      1. Now?
        (Can we add internal/external help? Are there people that wait for the results and thus have free time with which they could help?)
      2. In the long term?
        Education, Teachers, Hiring, Automation?
