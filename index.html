Continuous Developer Training

This repository/website tries to create a space for continuous training that you can utilise whenever you feel it is the right time to learn or relearn some development skill.

It contains example projects with lists of requirements, references to interesting books and videos as well as other websites.

Not only is it a collection but it also contains little random selectors. So when you find yourself in a situation where you can train alone or in pair programming for a little bit of time you can simply press a button and something will be proposed. Maybe a test project, maybe a technology, maybe an article ...
